About Beluga


Beluga is a company founded by Waha FZCO. Waha is a family owned corporation founded in 2022 in Dubai by married couple Hossam Taher and Katherine Siess. They focus on creating technology solutions that are sector agnostic. Beluga is a technology solution that provides multiple productivity and finance support for individuals and includes features like a password generator, a wealth manager, a liability and debt organizer, and a personal accounting tool. The name Beluga is inspired by their daughter's favorite song by the Egyptian 75 years old singer Raffi called (Baby Beluga).
